About Cradle

Helping students find homes near AAU Ekpoma

Cradle is a student housing platform built specifically for students of Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma. We connect students looking for accommodation with trusted agents and landlords near campus.

Our mission is simple — make finding a home near AAU stress-free, safe, and affordable.

Whether you're a fresh student looking for your first room or an agent with properties to list, Cradle is built for you.
